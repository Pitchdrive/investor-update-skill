# Investor update — section skeleton

> Delete any section that doesn't apply this month. Keep the order. Numbers over
> adjectives. Bad news first, not buried. End with asks specific enough to answer
> from a phone.

## 01 · CEO update
- The single most important fact of the month, first sentence.
- Last update I said: X → what happened (done / partial / missed, one line each).
- The story of the month, 3–6 paragraphs, first person.
- The one thing I'm focused on next month.

## 02 · Financial update
- Monthly revenue, last 6 months (table). MoM %.
- Gross margin, EBITDA or net burn.
- Cash position, runway in months.
- One paragraph of interpretation. If it was a bad month, say so in sentence one.

## 03 · Product
- What shipped, why it matters, early results (measured, dated).
- Structural changes as a small table: what changed / why.

## 04 · Engineering & velocity
- Shipped issues / releases / epics, team size.
- What this unlocks next quarter. Tie velocity to customer value.

## 05 · Team
- Headcount now → next month. Key hires/departures.
- Hiring plan or freeze, with reasoning.

## 06 · Culture & operating model *(occasional)*
- Only when something structural changed.

## 07 · Strategy
- The bet we're making, in two paragraphs.
- The 2–3 north-star metrics that will prove it right or wrong.

## 08 · Forecast & asks
- Coming months: actuals vs projections, clearly separated.
- Fundraise timing and sizing, when relevant.
- **Asks** (specific, answerable): intros to {named funds / profiles}, candidates
  for {role}, customers who {exact ICP}.
